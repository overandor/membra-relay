# MEMBRA Relay Pricing and Payment Splits

## Distance-Based Pricing

| Distance | Price Range |
|----------|--------------|
| Pickup only | $0 |
| Meet halfway | $2–$5 |
| Under 0.5 mile delivery | $4–$7 |
| 0.5–2 miles | $7–$12 |
| 2–5 miles | $12–$25 |
| Cross-city | Quote required |

## Surcharges

| Condition | Surcharge |
|-----------|-----------|
| Heavy/bulky items | +$3–$10 |
| Fragile items | +$2–$5 |
| High-value items ($100+) | +$5 or insurance required |
| Perishable items | +$2–$5 |
| Restricted items | +$5–$15 |
| Time-sensitive (under 30 min) | +$5–$15 |
| After-hours (9pm–7am) | +$5–$10 |
| Weekend delivery | +$3–$5 |

User pays: $18

## Minimum Payouts

To ensure fair compensation:
- Minimum agent payout: $4
- Minimum hub fee: $1
- Minimum inventory owner payout: $2

## Dynamic Pricing Factors

AI dispatch considers these factors to adjust pricing:
- Demand surge (peak hours): +10–30%
- Low supply (few agents): +15–25%
- Weather conditions (rain/snow): +10–20%
- Traffic conditions: +5–15%
- Route complexity (multiple stops): +5–10%
- Item risk level: +5–20%

## Tips and Bonuses

- Optional tips go 100% to the Relay Agent
- Performance bonuses for on-time delivery: +$1–$3
- Batch route efficiency bonus: +$2–$5 per additional item
- Referral bonus for new agents: $5

## Insurance

For high-value items:
- Items under $50: No insurance required
- Items $50–$200: $1 insurance reserve
- Items $200–$500: $3 insurance reserve
- Items $500+: 1% of value as insurance reserve

Insurance covers:
- Loss or damage during transit
- Theft
- Delivery to wrong address

## Proof Requirements

For payouts to be released:
- Photo of item at pickup location
- Photo of item at dropoff location
- GPS coordinates verification
- Optional signature for high-value items
- Timestamp verification

## Refund Policy

- Cancellation by sender before pickup: Full refund
- Cancellation after pickup but before delivery: 50% refund
- Failed delivery (agent fault): Full refund
- Failed delivery (sender fault, e.g., wrong address): No refund
- Item not as described: Full refund + return shipping

## Platform Fees

MEMBRA platform fees cover:
- Payment processing
- Insurance pool
- Dispatch AI
- Trust and verification
- Customer support
- Network infrastructure

Fee structure:
- Standard: 11–20% of total price
- Enterprise partners: Negotiated rates
- High-volume agents: Reduced rates (10–15%)
